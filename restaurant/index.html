Ammamma Vantillu

Ammamma Vantillu stands out for its commitment to authenticity and tradition. The restaurant embraces the rich culinary heritage, offering dishes that carry the timeless flavors of home-cooked meals passed down through generations.The menu at Ammamma Vantillu is a celebration of diverse and delectable flavors. From aromatic biryanis to savory dosas, each dish is carefully crafted with love and attention to detail, ensuring a memorable culinary journey for every guest.

 

The commitment to quality is evident in the use of fresh, locally sourced ingredients. Ammamma Vantillu takes pride in providing dishes that not only taste exceptional but also reflect a dedication to using the best possible components.The ambiance at Ammamma Vantillu is warm and inviting, creating a welcoming space for diners. The rustic charm, combined with the heartwarming smile of Ammamma herself, makes every visit a delightful experience.

 

Ammamma Vantillu is more than just a restaurant; it is a haven of tradition, flavors, and heartfelt hospitality that leaves a lasting impression on anyone fortunate enough to dine there.

Starters

Masala Vada - Deep-fried lentil patties with spices. Vegetable Bonda - Spiced and deep-fried mixed vegetable fritters. Crispy Curry Leaves - Crispy fried curry leaves with a hint of spice and more ....

Soups

Rasam - Tangy and spicy South Indian soup with tamarind and tomatoes. Kosambari - Refreshing salad with lentils, cucumber, and carrots. Coconut and Lemon Soup - Light soup with coconut milk and a touch of lemon and more ....

Side Dishes

Pudina Pachadi - Mint chutney with yogurt. Ginger Garlic Thokku - Condiment made with ginger, garlic, and spices. Coconut Chutney. Tomato Chutney. Mango Pickle and more ....

Main Courses

Puliyodarai - Tamarind rice with a blend of spices and peanuts. Pesarattu - Green gram dosa served with ginger chutney. Karuvadam Paruppu Kuzhambu - Tangy lentil curry with black gram fritters and more ....

Desserts

Kesari - Semolina pudding with ghee, sugar, and saffron. Payasam - Rice and milk pudding with cardamom and cashews. Mysore Pak - Sweet, dense, and melt-in-the-mouth fudge. Badam Halwa - Rich almond pudding and more ....

Beverages

Filter Coffee - Strong South Indian coffee served with milk. Buttermilk - Refreshing yogurt-based drink. Nannari Sherbet - Herbal drink made from the root of the Indian sarsaparilla plant. Solkadhi - Kokum-infused drink with coconut milk and more ....
